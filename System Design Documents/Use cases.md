# Use Cases - Durdans Hospital Clinic Management System

## Actors
*   **Patient**: The person seeking medical care.
*   **Doctor**: The medical professional providing care.
*   **Receptionist**: Staff member managing front-desk operations.
*   **Administrator**: Manages the system configuration and users.

## Core Use Cases

#### Current Scope

### 1. Patient Management (Current Scope)
*   **Register Patient**: Create a new patient profile with personal details (Name, DOB, Contact).
*   **Search Patient**: Find existing patients by name or phone number.
*   **Update Patient Details**: Modify contact info or medical history.

### 2. Doctor Management (Current Scope)
*   **Register Doctor**: Add a new doctor to the system with specialization and consultation fee.
*   **Manage Availability**: Set days and times when the doctor is available for consultations and time slots in a specific hospital.

### 3. Hospital Management (Current Scope)
*   **Register Hospital**: Add a new hospital to the system with name and address.
*   **Manage Hospital**: Update hospital details.

### 4. Appointment Management (Current Scope)
*   **Search Availability**: Find doctors based on Doctor name, specialization, Hospital Name and available time slots.
*   **Book Appointment**: Schedule a visit for a patient with a specific doctor.
*   **Cancel/Reschedule**: Modify an existing appointment.
*   **View Schedule**: Doctors can view their upcoming appointments.

#### Future Scope

### 5. Clinical Management (Future Scope)
*   **Record Consultation**: Doctor enters diagnosis and prescription notes.
*   **Order Lab Tests**: Doctor requests specific lab investigations.

### 6. Billing (Future Scope)
*   **Generate Invoice**: Calculate total cost (Doctor Fee + Hospital Fee).
*   **Process Payment**: Record payment method and status.
